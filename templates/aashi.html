The mehndi mixture is made from a ground paste or powder from the leaves of the henna plant. While henna can refer to the plant itself, mehndi almost always refers to the finished product of henna powder is used even for the art.

Tree Tree Oil

Tea tree oil is a popular addition to mehndi (henna) paste to achieve a darker, richer stain by releasing more dye, improving paste consistency for smoother application with fine lines, soothing skin and that traps heat for deep color.
