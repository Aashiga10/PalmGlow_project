About Palm Glow

Palm Glow is a dedicated mehndi art page founded and managed by Aashiga, created with a deep love for traditional artistry and modern design aesthetics.

Palm Glow was established with the vision of bringing elegant, meaningful, and beautifully detailed mehndi designs to every celebration. Each design is crafted with care, creativity, and cultural respect.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality mehndi designs that enhance the beauty of hands while preserving tradition, creativity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and recognized mehndi art brand known for creativity, hygiene, and artistic excellence.

What We Offer
  • ✔ Bridal Mehndi
  • ✔ Engagement & Wedding Events
  • ✔ Festival & Party Mehandi
  • ✔ Customized & Minimal Designs
  • ✔ Home & Venue Services
Quality & Safety

We use 100% natural henna made from organic ingredients. Hygiene and safety are always prioritized to ensure a comfortable and safe experience.
